Salesforce

Beginner to Intermediate

Overview

Salesforce is a cloud-based platform that provides software and services to create relevant customer experiences. Starting as Software-as-a-service(SaaS), Salesforce is based on a multi-tenant architecture that has benefits such as API Integration, configuration, scalability, free capacity, low-cost ownership, platform support, and more.

What you'll learn

  • Organize and manage customer and prospect data
  • Use Salesforce's sales and marketing tools to automate and streamline processes
  • Use analytics and reporting tools to track the effectiveness of your sales and marketing efforts
  • Work with data and reports to make informed decisions
  • Task management, Activity tracking, and Document sharing.

Requirements

  • Desktop / Laptop
  • Internet Connectivity (>2Mbps)

Target Audience

  • Sales reps
  • Customer service reps
  • CRM managers
  • Marketing managers
